JM&C Alumni

JM&C alumni are everywhere.

From covering the NBA playoffs and the Summer Olympics for The Associated Press to developing and managing the social media platforms for the Nebraska Humane Society, JM&C alums are reporters, editors and photographers. They are programmers, developers and network administrators.

They create films and advertising campaigns. They work for newspapers, TV stations, business-to-business magazines, consumer magazines, Fortune 500 companies, federal and local governments and for nonprofits, large and small. They lead as company presidents and work as attorneys and public defenders.

They design and strategize and communicate. Some teach in high school and middle school, encouraging the next generation of writers and journalists.

Stay up-to-date with our alumni through social media and our alumni list-serv.
